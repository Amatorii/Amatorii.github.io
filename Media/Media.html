Here is my Portfolio for 3/4 Media. The Media product I worked on for the majority of the year is a video essay talking about how stories are told in video games. I mainly look at Dark Souls 3 and how through implicit story telling, it's able to tell it's story without forcing the players to play through it. My main contention that implicit story telling is a better convention than explicit as it gives the player more freedom and control.
